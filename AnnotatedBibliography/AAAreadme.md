# AAAreadme for Annotated Bilbiography

Annotated bibliographies are really important when working in a new field.

- Change the title.
- Enter the annote field in JabRef
- Copy the bibtex entry from JabRef to annot.bib.
- Write one sentence per line. The sentences will get line wrapped.
- Use two sets of double backspaces to introduce blank line between paragraphs.
- Be sure to save global.bib before copying the bibtex entry otherwise you will copy the old version.
- Tag the pdfs or epubs with a magenta dot on those that have been entered into annot.bib.
